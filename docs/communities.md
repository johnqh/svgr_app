# Designer Communities by Language

A curated list of active online communities for graphic designers, vector artists, logo designers, and image conversion enthusiasts. Organized by the 15 languages supported by the SVGR app.

---

## English (en)

### r/graphic_design

- **URL:** https://www.reddit.com/r/graphic_design/
- **Platform:** reddit
- **Description:** The largest Reddit community for graphic designers with over 2.1 million members, focused on visual communication and commercial art. Portfolio reviews, career advice, and design critiques.

### r/logodesign

- **URL:** https://www.reddit.com/r/logodesign/
- **Platform:** reddit
- **Description:** A subreddit with 226K+ members dedicated to logo design and branding. Members share logos for feedback and discuss logo design principles.

### r/vectorart

- **URL:** https://www.reddit.com/r/vectorart/
- **Platform:** reddit
- **Description:** A community for sharing and discussing vector art created with tools like Adobe Illustrator, Inkscape, and other vector graphics software. Covers SVG techniques and vector design.

### Design Buddies

- **URL:** https://discord.com/invite/designbuddies
- **Platform:** discord
- **Description:** Discord's largest design community with 98,000+ members. Offers workshops, talks with industry leaders, mentorship, portfolio reviews, job board, and networking for designers at all levels.

### Graphic Design Discord

- **URL:** https://discord.com/invite/graphicdesign
- **Platform:** discord
- **Description:** Discord's premier design community with 47,000+ members. Features a daily marketplace for clients and designers, regular contests, and welcomes all creatives including graphic design, 3D, UI/UX, video, and illustration.

### Dribbble

- **URL:** https://dribbble.com/
- **Platform:** other
- **Description:** A leading platform where the design community connects, shares shots of ongoing work, and gets inspired. Ideal for quick visual showcases, networking, and finding design opportunities.

### Behance

- **URL:** https://www.behance.net/
- **Platform:** other
- **Description:** The world's largest creative network by Adobe for showcasing and discovering creative work. Supports full project case studies, process breakdowns, and long-form portfolio presentation.
