Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-02-14

Introduction

Sudobility Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") operates SVGR, a raster-to-vector image conversion service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected

  • Browser type and version
  • Language preference
  • Anonymous usage analytics (page views, feature usage)

User-Provided Content

  • Images you upload for conversion (temporarily retained on our servers)
  • Conversion settings (quality, transparency preferences)

Conversion History (Authenticated Users)

  • Converted SVG files and associated metadata (timestamps, file dimensions, conversion settings)
  • Thumbnail previews derived from your uploaded images
  • Account identifiers linking conversion history to your account

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the image conversion service
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize performance
  • To maintain the security and integrity of our service
  • To provide authenticated users access to their conversion history

Data Storage & Retention

Uploaded images are temporarily retained on our servers for processing. For authenticated users, conversion results and metadata are stored as part of your conversion history and retained until you delete them or delete your account. Conversion history data is not shared with third parties. Anonymous (unauthenticated) conversions are not stored beyond the active session.

Third-Party Services

We use Firebase for hosting and analytics. Firebase may collect anonymous usage data in accordance with Google's privacy policy. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

  • Request information about the data we collect
  • Request deletion of any data associated with you
  • Opt out of analytics tracking by using browser privacy settings or ad blockers

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
