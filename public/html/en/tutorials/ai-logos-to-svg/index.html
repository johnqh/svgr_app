Convert AI Logos to SVG

All AI logo generators — Looka, Brandmark, Hatchful, Logo.com, and others — output raster images (PNG or JPG). Raster logos lose quality when scaled up for banners, merchandise, or print. They appear blurry on high-DPI screens and cannot be edited cleanly in vector design tools like Illustrator or Figma. To use an AI-generated logo professionally, you need a scalable vector (SVG) version.

How to Convert

  1. Upload your AI-generated logo image (PNG, JPG, or WEBP).
  2. Set Image Type to Design. This tells SVGR to preserve hard edges and flat color regions typical of logo artwork.
  3. Set Quality Level to 5. This captures fine detail without over-complicating the SVG paths. You can adjust up or down depending on the complexity of your logo.
  4. Click Convert and preview the result.
  5. Download the SVG.

Tips

  • Use a logo image with a transparent or solid background for the cleanest result.
  • If the result looks too simplified, increase the quality level. If it looks too complex, decrease it.
  • The SVG output is a great starting point for designers to fine-tune in Illustrator or Figma — even if it is not pixel-perfect.
